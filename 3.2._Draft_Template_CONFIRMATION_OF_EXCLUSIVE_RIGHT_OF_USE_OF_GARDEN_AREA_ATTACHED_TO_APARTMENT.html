The Developer hereby CONFIRMS as follows by this instrument:
Article 1. Confirmation of Exclusive Garden Area Use
In accordance with the design and planning approved by the competent State authority, Apartment No. ..., Floor ..., Block ..., of the ... project (hereinafter "Apartment") is allocated an external floor area designed and constructed for garden use (hereinafter "Garden Area").
The area, location, boundaries, shape, and current condition of the Garden Area are specifically set out [in the as-built drawing] in the Appendix attached to this Confirmation, which is an inseparable part of this instrument.
Article 2. Legal Nature of the Right to Use the Garden Area
The Garden Area is not an area for which a Certificate of private ownership is issued and is not part of the common ownership of the apartment building.
The Developer confirms that the exclusive right of use over the Garden Area is a right attached to the Apartment, arising from the design, planning, and spatial use arrangement of the project.
The exclusive right of use over the Garden Area:
  • Is not a right arising from a tenancy, loan, gratuitous use, or conditional permission arrangement;
  • Is not dependent on the existence, validity, or termination of any SPA that has been or will be executed.
Article 3. Principle of Transfer of the Right to Use the Garden Area
The exclusive right of use over the Garden Area automatically transfers with the ownership of the Apartment.
When the Apartment is lawfully transferred to a new owner, the new owner of the Apartment automatically and entirely inherits the right to use the Garden Area, without needing to:
  • Execute any contract, agreement, or instrument with the Developer;
  • Obtain the consent, confirmation, or participation of the Developer or any third party.
The right to use the Garden Area may not be separated from, or transferred independently of, the Apartment in any form.
Article 4. Duration of the Right to Use the Garden Area
The exclusive right of use over the Garden Area is effective for the entire duration of the lawful existence and operation of the project, in accordance with the land allocation/lease decision of the competent State authority.
Adjustment, restriction, or termination of the right to use the Garden Area may only be effected in the cases specified in Article 6 of this Confirmation.
Article 5. Independent Validity of this Confirmation
This Confirmation has the effect of confirming and establishing the exclusive right of use over the Garden Area attached to the Apartment, and has legal force independently of the SPAs for the apartment.
This Confirmation shall continue in effect even in the event that:
  • The Developer has completed all construction and development obligations;
  • The Developer ceases operations, dissolves, is declared bankrupt, or transfers its rights and obligations in accordance with applicable law.
Article 6. Absolute Limit on Management Authority over the Right to Use the Garden Area
The exclusive right of use over the Garden Area does not fall within the scope of management and disposal by the apartment building community.
The Management Board of the apartment building, the management and operation entity, the Apartment Building Conference, and any organization or individual participating in the management and operation of the apartment building, are not entities with authority to decide on the withdrawal, termination, restriction, change of use purpose, or change of legal nature of the right to use the Garden Area.
Any resolution, decision, meeting minutes, vote, agreement, or other expression of intent by the entities referred to in Clause 2 of this Article that purports to affect the right to use the Garden Area shall have no legal effect and shall not give rise to, alter, or extinguish the right to use the Garden Area.
The right to use the Garden Area may only be adjusted or terminated in the following cases:
  • A decision of the competent State authority in accordance with applicable law; or
  • A mandatory provision of law directly applicable to the project.
Article 7. Final Provisions
This Confirmation is issued to record the legal status of the right to use the Garden Area attached to the Apartment and to serve as the basis for protecting the legitimate rights and interests of the lawful owners of the Apartment for the entire project term.
This Confirmation takes effect from the date of signing.
